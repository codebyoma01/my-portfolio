My Story

Self-taught.
Detail-obsessed.
Builder.

I'm Ijeoma known online as Code by Oma. I'm a self-taught Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer based in Abuja, Nigeria, and I build websites that businesses are proud to share.

My path into tech wasn't traditional. I taught myself to code from scratch, starting with the fundamentals of HTML and CSS and working my way up to JavaScript, React, and Next.js. Along the way I discovered that I cared just as much about how a product looks and feels as how it works so design became as central to my practice as development.

That combination is rare. Most developers hand off to a designer. Most designers hand off to a developer. I do both which means the layout you approve is the layout you ship, with nothing lost in translation.

I work with businesses and startups across Nigeria and beyond, building everything from landing pages and e-commerce stores to full web applications and product dashboards. My clients come back and they send referrals because I care about the details that most people skip: spacing that breathes, motion that has a reason, and code that holds up on every screen size.
