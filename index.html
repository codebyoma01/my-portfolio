About

Design thinking,
written in code.

I'm a self-taught developer and designer based in Abuja, Nigeria. My work sits at the intersection of two disciplines most people separate I design interfaces in Figma, then build them myself, pixel by pixel, in code.

That combination means nothing gets lost in translation between design and development. The layout you approve is the layout you ship. I care about the details that make a site feel premium: spacing that breathes, motion that has a reason to exist, and code that loads fast and holds up on every screen size.
